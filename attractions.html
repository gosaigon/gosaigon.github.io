🛵 Cost of Motorbike Taxis in Saigon

Motorbike taxis (GrabBike, Gojek) are the fastest way to explore Saigon. Here’s what you can expect to pay:

  • Short rides (under 3 km): 12,000–20,000 VND ($0.50–$0.80)
  • Medium rides (3–7 km): 20,000–40,000 VND ($0.80–$1.60)
  • Longer trips (7–15 km): 40,000–80,000 VND ($1.60–$3.20)

💡 Tip: Prices may rise during rush hour (7–9 AM, 5–7 PM) or rainy weather.
