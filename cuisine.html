🍽️ Food in Saigon

🥢 What Saigonites Eat

Vietnamese cuisine is vibrant and full of bold flavors—salty, sweet, sour, spicy—balanced with fresh herbs, savory broths, and lively street-side scenes. Must-try dishes include Phở, Cơm tấm, Bánh mì, Bún thịt nướng, and Bánh xèo.

🥗 Vegetarian & Vegan Favorites

  • Hum Vegetarian (32 Võ Văn Tần, D3): Zen setting, creative dishes 🌿
  • Chay 3 La (32A Cao Ba Nha, D1): Leaf-based dishes, 35–100k VND 🌿
  • Loving Hut Hoa Đăng (63 Mai Thị Lựu, D1): Vegan, 6–9 USD 🌿
  • Prem Bistro (D3): Soups, salads, light fusion 🌿

Vegetarian food is often 50% cheaper and just as delicious as meat dishes. Try phở chay, bánh xèo chay, and cơm tấm chay for flavorful options.
