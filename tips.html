River & Sea Travel

Saigon Waterbus

A scenic public ferry along the Saigon River. It's a fantastic way to see the city skyline and escape the traffic. The main route connects Bach Dang Pier (D1) with Linh Dong (Thu Duc City).

Price: ~15,000 VND per trip

Best for: Sightseeing & relaxed travel

Ferry to Vung Tau

For a quick beach getaway, the high-speed ferry is the most comfortable and scenic option. The journey takes about 2 hours, offering great views as you leave the city.

Operator: Greenlines DP

Departs from: Bach Dang Pier, D1

Getting to Con Dao Islands

Reaching this stunning archipelago requires a multi-step journey. The fastest way is a direct flight from HCMC. Alternatively, take a bus to Vung Tau or Soc Trang, then a high-speed ferry (4-6 hours) to the island.

Sea Route: Bus + Ferry (Con Dao Express)

Fastest Route: Direct Flight (SGN Airport)

The Future is Here: Saigon's Metro

Line 1: Now Operational!

The first line of the HCMC Metro, Line 1, is now open! It connects the city center with the eastern suburbs, providing a fast, modern, and air-conditioned way to travel. The line runs from Ben Thanh station in District 1 to Suoi Tien Terminal in Thu Duc City.

Future Expansion

Line 1 is just the beginning. The master plan for Ho Chi Minh City includes a network of 8 metro lines, monorails, and light rail routes that will eventually connect all corners of the city, transforming public transport and making it easier than ever to explore.
